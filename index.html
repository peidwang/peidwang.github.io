Current Research Project

Summer time formaldehyde (HCHO) diurnal cycle from CMAQ model

I started doing research with Prof. Tracey Holloway at UW-Madison on atmospheric science since Fall 2016. Currently I am focusing on assessing the uncertainty of satellite in observing ambient formaldehyde (HCHO) in the U.S. over the past 10 years. I am also comparing satellite retrievals with in situ measurements, numerical models, and emission reports to explain HCHO trends.
